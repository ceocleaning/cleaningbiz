How It Works

Meet Sarah, Your AI Voice Agent

Sarah handles all your customer calls, collects information, provides quotes, and schedules appointments - all while sounding completely human.

1

Customer Calls Your Business

When a customer calls, Sarah answers professionally with your business name and begins the conversation.

2

Collects Customer Information

Sarah gathers customer details (name, contact info) and property information (size, rooms) to provide accurate quotes.

3

Provides Service Options and Pricing

Based on gathered information, Sarah explains your service options and provides transparent pricing.

4

Schedules Appointments

Sarah checks your availability and books appointments within your business hours (9 AM - 5 PM Central Time).

5

Sends Confirmations

After booking, Sarah sends confirmation details to both you and your customer, ensuring everyone is informed.

Workflow

How It Works

Understand the automated workflow that powers your cleaning business

1. Lead Generation

Leads are automatically captured from Thumbtack via webhook integration or can be manually added to the system.

Automatic lead data collection from Thumbtack

Manual lead entry option available

2. Initial Contact

The system automatically sends an SMS to the lead's phone number to initiate contact.

Automated SMS sent immediately to new leads

SMS AI Agent handles text conversations

3. Follow-up Call

If the lead doesn't respond to the SMS within 5 minutes, the system automatically triggers an outbound call using Retell AI.

Automated outbound calling via Retell

Natural voice conversation with potential clients

4. Booking Process

When a lead shows interest, the AI agent collects all necessary details and creates a booking in the system.

Collection of property details (square footage, bedrooms, bathrooms)

Service selection based on your configured pricing

Scheduling based on cleaner availability

5. Payment Processing

After booking confirmation, an invoice is automatically generated and shared with the client for payment.

Automatic invoice generation

Secure payment processing via Square

Option for instant payment or pre-authorization

Everything you need to know about CleaningBiz AI

CleaningBiz AI is an intelligent virtual assistant platform specifically designed for cleaning businesses. It combines advanced AI technology with industry-specific knowledge to automate customer interactions, lead capture, appointment scheduling, and payment processing.

Our AI assistant, Sarah, can handle phone calls, answer customer questions, provide quotes, and book appointments - all while sounding completely human and representing your brand professionally.

Most cleaning businesses can complete the entire setup process in under 2 hours. The basic account creation takes just minutes, while integrating with third-party services and configuring your AI assistant's personality and services might take a bit longer.

Our step-by-step guide walks you through each part of the process, and our support team is available to help if you encounter any issues.

No technical skills are required! CleaningBiz AI is designed to be user-friendly for cleaning business owners regardless of technical background. Our setup wizard guides you through each step with clear instructions and visual aids.

For the integrations with services like Square and Thumbtack, we provide direct links and step-by-step instructions. If you ever need help, our support team can assist you via chat or schedule a screen-sharing session to walk you through any challenging steps.

CleaningBiz AI offers flexible pricing plans starting at $99/month for our Basic plan, which includes up to 100 AI-handled calls per month. Our Professional plan at $199/month includes up to 300 calls and additional features like priority support and advanced analytics.

For larger cleaning businesses, our Enterprise plan offers custom pricing with unlimited calls and dedicated account management. All plans include a 14-day free trial so you can experience the full benefits before committing.

The CleaningBiz AI subscription covers all core platform features. However, there may be additional costs from third-party services:

  • Retell AI: Requires a separate subscription (starting at $49/month) for voice AI capabilities
  • Square: Standard payment processing fees apply (typically 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction)
  • Thumbtack: Standard lead fees apply based on your Thumbtack plan
  • Phone number: If you choose to get a new dedicated number rather than using your existing business line

We're transparent about these costs during the setup process, and many businesses find that the time saved and increased booking rates more than offset these expenses.

Sarah, our AI assistant, can handle a wide range of customer interactions:

  • Answer common questions about your services
  • Collect customer information (name, address, contact details)
  • Gather property details (square footage, number of rooms, etc.)
  • Provide accurate quotes based on your pricing structure
  • Schedule appointments within your business hours
  • Process payments and send confirmation details
  • Follow up with leads who haven't booked
  • Handle appointment rescheduling and cancellations

Sarah is trained specifically for the cleaning industry and understands common terminology, service types, and customer concerns.

While our AI assistant is highly capable, there may be complex situations it can't handle. In these cases:

  • The AI will politely explain that it needs to transfer the call to a human team member
  • You'll receive an immediate notification via your preferred method (SMS, email, or app alert)
  • If you're available, you can take over the call directly
  • If you're unavailable, the AI will take a detailed message and schedule a callback
  • All information collected before the transfer is saved and accessible to you

Over time, the AI learns from these situations and improves its handling of similar scenarios in the future.

Dynamic System Prompts

Our AI Voice Agent uses a sophisticated template system that automatically customizes conversations for your business:

  • Templates stored in database with placeholders for business details
  • Runtime substitution of company name, services, hours, and pricing
  • Intelligent fallbacks to defaults when specific settings aren't available 
// Dynamic prompt generation
function generatePrompt(business) {
  return template.replace(
    "{{company_name}}", 
    business.name || "our cleaning service"
  );
}
